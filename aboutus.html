About Us

We Are The Best At What We Do !

The idea is to develop an Online Student's Handy Guide with Small Scale Industry project. Users are privileged to look at the services offered and book their appointments faster. The project aims to build an application program to reduce the work a student has to do when coming to a new city looking for accommodation, food, transport, and more such services.

In the existing system, a student would have to scour the internet to look for accommodation, food, transport, and such services separately, which is time-consuming, fatiguing, and highly inefficient, to say the least.

This project strives to change that as "Efficiency is doing better what is already being done" ~Peter Drucker. The aim is to reduce the time it takes the student to settle in a new city by placing all the required services in a single place. An "all you need handbook of sorts" helps students through all their basic day-to-day needs.
